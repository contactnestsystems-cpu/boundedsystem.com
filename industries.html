Claims framing

NEST is a deterministic, non-adaptive system constraint. It does not rely on prediction, learning, or feedback. Its effects are structural: when enabled, certain categories of instability cannot propagate or sustain, regardless of domain. Observable improvements depend on context and workload; no performance or outcome guarantees are implied beyond bounded behavior and non-propagation of instability.
