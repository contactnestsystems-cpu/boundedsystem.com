Industries

NEST can be applied in many sectors where safety and predictability are critical. Examples include:

Manufacturing & Automation

Used in factories and production lines to ensure reliable operation of robots and machinery. NEST provides consistent performance under all conditions.

Energy & Utilities

Applied in power plants and grid management for fail-safe control. NEST helps prevent blackouts by containing faults within predetermined limits.

Transportation

Critical systems like rail signaling, traffic control, and vehicle automation benefit from NEST's deterministic behavior and certified safety guarantees.

Robotics & IoT

Used in robotics and embedded devices to maintain bounded performance. NEST ensures that connected devices act within known parameters, reducing risk.
