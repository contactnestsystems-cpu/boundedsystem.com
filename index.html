Welcome to NEST

NEST provides a deterministic, bounded approach to AI and autonomous control. Unlike typical predictive machine learning systems, NEST operates within predefined rules and limits to ensure predictable outcomes and fail-safe behavior. Explore our site to learn how NEST works and the changes it brings to safe automation.

Key features of NEST include:

  • Fully deterministic logic: fixed input yields fixed output.
  • Bounded behavior: all outcomes stay within safe ranges.
  • Modular containment: failures do not propagate beyond their module.
  • Non-adaptive architecture: no online learning or model updates in operation.
