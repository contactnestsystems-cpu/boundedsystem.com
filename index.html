NEXT-GEN RELIABILITY LAYER

Bound system behavior. Prevent instability from propagating.

NEST is a deterministic, non-adaptive system constraint. It does not rely on prediction, learning, or feedback. Its effects are structural: when enabled, certain categories of instability cannot propagate or sustain.

Bound worst-case

Precludes unbounded escalation under uncertainty.

  • Target: tail blow-ups, overload loops, unstable recovery.
  • Effect: bounded behavior instead of runaway amplification.
NP

Non-propagating

Keeps failures local; reduces blast radius.

  • Target: retry storms, queue avalanches, cascade chains.
  • Effect: propagation is structurally prevented from sustaining.
