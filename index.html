What NEST is

A deterministic constraint layer that operates above domain logic and before rule escalation. It constrains system evolution rather than outputs.

  • No prediction, no learning, no adaptive state.
  • Enforces bounded behavior under uncertainty.
  • Deterministic and repeatable by design.

What NEST changes

Instead of detecting failure after it happens, NEST structurally prevents certain categories of escalation from sustaining or propagating.

  • Containment of retry storms / queue avalanches.
  • Bounded recovery dynamics (less oscillation).
  • Lower systemic risk during disturbances.
