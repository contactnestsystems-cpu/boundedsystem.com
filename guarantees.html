Guarantees & Limits

Guarantees

  • Deterministic Execution: A given input will always produce the same output.
  • Bounded Outputs: All results are confined to predefined ranges, preventing outliers.
  • Error Containment: Any malfunction or exception is isolated within its module.
  • Auditability: No hidden adaptive behavior means the entire system can be inspected and verified.

Limitations

  • No Learning or Adaptation: NEST does not improve or change itself after deployment.
  • Fixed Domain: It operates only in the domains and scenarios it was explicitly programmed for.
  • Design Effort Required: Building a NEST system requires thorough upfront specification and validation.
  • Not Predictive: NEST does not predict future states based on statistical inference; it follows rules.
