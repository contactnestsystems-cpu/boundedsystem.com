Technology

NEST does not remove uncertainty. It constrains uncertainty’s ability to propagate and amplify by enforcing bounded action envelopes prior to execution.

What it does

  • Constrains propagation of instability and interference.
  • Caps impact under uncertainty through bounded authority.
  • Reduces safety-induced execution drag in governed systems.

What it does not do

  • No claim of correctness, prediction, or optimality.
  • No claim to replace domain controls or regulated services.
