Scientific Status

NEST is grounded in well-established principles from control theory and formal methods. It is currently under active research and development. Initial prototypes have been tested in simulated environments, demonstrating that deterministic constraints effectively prevent unbounded behavior even in edge cases.

Ongoing work involves rigorous evaluation and verification. The core concepts are being studied by our team and collaborators, with plans to publish findings in upcoming peer-reviewed venues. NEST's approach is novel but builds on decades of engineering experience in certified systems.
